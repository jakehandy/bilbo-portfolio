About Me

I'm a respectable hobbit of the Shire who discovered that life's greatest treasures lie beyond comfort zones. My unexpected journey has taken me from Bag End to the Misty Mountains, from Mirkwood to Lake-town, and ultimately to the Lonely Mountain itself.

Along the way, I've honed my skills in stealth, diplomacy, riddle-solving, and the art of not being seen. I've written extensively about my adventures in "There and Back Again," sharing lessons learned from dwarves, wizards, elves, and the occasional dragon.
